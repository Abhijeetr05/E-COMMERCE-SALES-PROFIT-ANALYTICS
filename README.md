# E-Commerce-Sales-Profit-Analytics
 ## Overview
This project presents an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends across multiple regions and categories.
The dashboard provides actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making in areas such as revenue growth, customer segmentation, and product optimization.

## Project Objective
To provide a comprehensive view of e-commerce performance by:
- Tracking key business KPIs
- Identifying high-performing products & regions
- Understanding customer segments
- Analyzing the impact of discounts on profitability

## Key Business Metrics
- Total Sales: $484.56K
- Total Profit: $158.87K
- Total Orders: 2K
- Profit Margin: 32.79%

## Key Insights
- Consumer segment drives ~55% of total sales
- Europe & North America lead in revenue generation
- Technology & Furniture categories are most profitable
- Higher discounts negatively impact profit margins
- Sales peak during Q4, especially October

## Dashboard Features
### Sales & Profit Trends
- Monthly comparison of revenue and profit
- Identification of peak and low-performing months
### Customer Segmentation
- Analysis by Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office
- Revenue contribution by segment
### Regional Analysis
- Country/region-wise performance breakdown
- Comparative visualization of global markets
### Product Performance
- Top products by sales, quantity, and profit
- Business-focused tabular insights
### Category-wise Profitability
- Deep dive into:
  - Clothing & Accessories
  - Furniture
  - Office Supplies
  - Technology
### Discount Impact Analysis
- Correlation between discount rates and profit
- Helps in pricing and promotion strategies
### Payment Filtering
- Insights based on:
   - Bank Transfer
   - Cash on Delivery
   - PayPal
   - Credit Card

## Tech Stack
- Power BI – Data visualization & dashboarding
- Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation
- DAX – Calculated measures & KPIs
- Dataset – E-commerce sample dataset
